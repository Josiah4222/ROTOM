Home-Based Senior Support

Creating a Caring and Supportive Home Environment for Our Seniors

Our Approach

ROTOM Ethiopia provides a range of home-based support programs designed to enhance the quality of life for 80 older persons in our community:

Psycho-Social Growth

Promoting emotional and social well-being through monthly meetings and supportive home visits.

Health Enhancement

Offering medical services, annual health check-ups, monthly sanitation supplies, and educational resources to improve overall health.

Income and Food Security

Providing monthly grocery support to ensure access to essential resources for older adults.

Educational Support for Dependents

Reducing the burden on older caregivers by supplying educational resources to empower the next generation.

Additional Support

Facilitating home repairs, holiday packages, clothing, and dignified burial assistance.

Awareness Raising

Advocating for the dignity and rights of older individuals.
